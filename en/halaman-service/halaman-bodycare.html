Bodycare

Body care practices are done to maintain the health and beauty of the skin, hair, and nails. Body care can also help relieve stress and improve mental health.

Body Wash

A liquid body soap used to cleanse the body, removing dirt, dust, dead skin cells, and oil, while maintaining skin moisture.

Packaging Variations Bottle or Tube : 10 mL, 15 mL, 20 mL, 50 mL

Body Lotion

Body lotion is a skincare product that functions to moisturize the skin. It can be used for the entire body, except the face.

Packaging Variations Serum Bottle / Dropper Bottle : 5ml, 10ml, 15ml, 20ml, 30ml

Body Oil

Body oil is a skincare product based on natural oils, like jojoba, olive, or almond, designed to moisturize and nourish the body's skin, and help lock in natural moisture.

Packaging Variations Bottle or Tube : 10 mL, 15 mL, 20 mL, 50 mL

Body Scrub

A body scrub is a skincare product that exfoliates dead skin cells, dirt, and excess oil. It can leave the skin feeling smoother, looking brighter, and healthier.

Packaging Variations POT : 10 ml, 15 ml, 20 ml
