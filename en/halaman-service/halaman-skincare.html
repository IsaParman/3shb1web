Skincare

A series of care products used to maintain the health and appearance of the skin, typically from the face to the neck. It comes in various product variants that can be tailored to different skin problems and types. Formulated with high-quality active ingredients that are beneficial for the facial skin.

Acne Spot

A skincare product specifically for treating acne by applying it only to the affected area. This product is practical for calming sudden acne-induced inflammation. It comes in cream, lotion, and gel textures and contains active ingredients to reduce acne and fade scars.

Packaging Variations Bottle or Tube : 10 mL, 15 mL, 20 mL, 50 mL

Serum is a skincare product with a light texture and a high concentration of active ingredients, designed to address specific skin issues and provide intensive care.
