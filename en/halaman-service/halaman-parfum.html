Perfume

Fragrance is a blend of scents created to add a pleasant and unique touch. Not just in perfumes, fragrances are also often found in cosmetics, soaps, and various other products, making the user's experience more special.

Eau De Toilette

Eau de Toilette (EDT) is a lighter concentration of perfume, suitable for everyday use. With an essential oil content of about 5-15%, EDT provides a lighter, pleasant scent that typically lasts for 3-4 hours.

Packaging Variations

Eau De Parfum

Eau de Parfum (EDP) is a perfume variant with a higher concentration than Eau de Toilette, providing a stronger and longer-lasting scent. With an essential oil content between 15-20%, EDP offers a more intense aroma experience and can last up to 6-8 hours.

Packaging Variations

Extrait De Parfum

Extrait de Parfum is the most potent and long-lasting perfume concentration. With an essential oil content of about 20-30%, this variant offers an exceptionally intense scent that can last for 24 hours or even more.

Packaging Variations Bottle or Tube : 10 mL, 15 mL, 20 mL, 50 mL
