Decorative

Decorative or make-up products are cosmetics designed to beautify the face and other parts of the body. From lipstick to foundation, each item has a special role in highlighting features and boosting user confidence.

Lip Tint

Lip tint is a cosmetic product for the lips that provides a color effect similar to lipstick, but with a lighter texture and a more natural finish. In liquid or gel form, lip tint is easy to apply with an applicator, resulting in a fresh and long-lasting look.

Packaging Variations

Foundation

Foundation is a cosmetic product that serves to conceal blemishes or imperfections on the face, creating a more even and smooth skin appearance.

Packaging Variations

Lipstick

Lipstick is a cosmetic used to add color, moisturize, and protect the lips. Lipstick can also be used to enhance facial aesthetics.

Packaging Variations Bottle or Tube : 10 mL, 15 mL, 20 mL, 50 mL

BB Cream

One type of light and refreshing body fragrance for short-term use. The composition of this fragrance generally consists of water and alcohol. It is a liquid that can be sprayed all over the body.

Packaging Variations POT : 10 ml, 15 ml, 20 ml
